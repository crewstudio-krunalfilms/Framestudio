The full picture

How FrameStudio works

From the moment you sign up to the moment your couple downloads their photos — here's exactly what happens.

For you, the studio

Connect Drive once

You approve a one-time Google permission. From then on, every gallery you create automatically gets its own folder in your Drive — named after the couple — and every photo you upload goes straight there.

📁
Drive/FrameStudio/Rohan & Priya
Auto-created when gallery is made
Photos & films

Upload anything, play anywhere

Drag in photos and videos — they upload directly to Drive with a progress bar. Got a film on YouTube or Vimeo instead? Just paste the link — it plays right inside the gallery, no redirect.

📸 JPG / PNG / RAW 🎬 MP4 / MOV ▶ YouTube link ▶ Vimeo link
For the couple

Just view and download

No account needed. Your couple opens their link, enters the password if you set one, and lands straight in their gallery. They can like photos, download them, and share the link with family — all without signing up for anything.
